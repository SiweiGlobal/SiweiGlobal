## Why I Started SiweiGlobal

The name **SIWEI** comes from the Chinese idea:

> 居安思危

It means remembering uncertainty even during peaceful and stable moments.

Life does not always follow the plans we make.

Because of my personal circumstances, I became aware of this uncertainty earlier than many people. I learned that time, health, opportunity, and stability should never be taken for granted.

That awareness did not make me want to stop.

It made me want to create.

I want to build useful things, document what I learn, create products with character, and leave behind work that proves I was here and that I tried.

SiweiGlobal was created from that belief.

## What I Am Building

The project includes:

- Original text and screenshot preservation
- Multilingual RFQ input
- Structured information extraction
- Customer information management
- RFQ analysis
- Long-term website storage

## Principles

### Build Before Expanding

Finish the current system before adding unrelated functions.

### Preserve Original Information

Original data, text, images, and records should remain intact.

### Document the Process

Important decisions should not exist only in memory.

### Keep Projects Independent

Each project should have a clear purpose and boundary.

### Create Things That Last

Products may be sold, websites may change, and businesses may fail.

But ideas, documentation, code, and sincere work can remain.
