# 2026 年 7 月：阿里巴巴国际站与 RFQ 项目

## 本月完成的工作

### 开通阿里巴巴国际站

7 月开通了阿里巴巴国际站店铺，开始进行店铺和商品内容的建设。

### 上传首饰商品

店铺上传的商品以首饰为主，主要包括：

- 耳钉
- 项链
- 吊坠
- 首饰成品
- 首饰半成品

这一阶段的重点是建立基础商品内容，让店铺具备初步的产品展示和客户询盘承接能力。

### 创建 RFQ 项目

店铺开通后，创建了 RFQ 项目，用于记录阿里巴巴国际站 RFQ 的报价信息，并对这些信息进行长期存储和分析。

项目当前关注的内容包括：

- 保存 RFQ 原始信息
- 记录报价相关内容
- 对 RFQ 信息进行整理和归档
- 为后续查询、比较和分析保留数据基础

## 本月形成的工作方向

7 月的工作形成了两个相互关联的方向：

1. 通过阿里巴巴国际站展示首饰产品并接触海外需求。
2. 通过 RFQ 项目保存和分析报价信息，逐步积累可供长期使用的市场记录。

## 下一步

- 继续完善店铺商品信息
- 持续记录新的 RFQ 和报价内容
- 根据实际使用情况完善 RFQ 的存储与分析方式
- 从积累的信息中观察客户需求和产品机会

# July 2026: Alibaba.com and the RFQ Project

## Work Completed This Month

### Opened an Alibaba.com Store

In July, I opened an Alibaba.com store and began building its basic store and product content.

### Uploaded Jewelry Products

The products uploaded to the store are mainly jewelry, including:

- Stud earrings
- Necklaces
- Pendants
- Finished jewelry
- Semi-finished jewelry components

The focus at this stage is to establish the basic product catalog so that the store can present its products and begin receiving customer inquiries.

### Created the RFQ Project

After opening the store, I created the RFQ project to record quotation information from Alibaba.com RFQs and to store and analyze that information over time.

The project currently focuses on:

- Preserving original RFQ information
- Recording quotation details
- Organizing and archiving RFQ records
- Building a data foundation for future searches, comparisons, and analysis

## Direction Established This Month

The work in July established two connected directions:

1. Present jewelry products and learn about overseas demand through Alibaba.com.
2. Preserve and analyze quotation information through the RFQ project, gradually building a long-term record of the market.

## Next Steps

- Continue improving product information in the store
- Continue recording new RFQs and quotation details
- Refine the RFQ storage and analysis methods based on actual use
- Observe customer needs and product opportunities within the accumulated information
