# 阿里巴巴国际站店铺

## 项目背景

2026 年 7 月，开通阿里巴巴国际站店铺，开始通过国际 B2B 平台展示首饰产品并接触海外客户需求。

## 项目目标

- 建立首饰产品的线上展示渠道
- 完善商品信息和店铺基础内容
- 接收询盘并了解海外市场需求
- 为后续的报价、客户沟通和产品调整积累经验

## 主要商品

目前上传的商品以首饰为主，包括：

- 耳钉
- 项链
- 吊坠
- 首饰成品
- 首饰半成品

## 当前状态

店铺已经开通，并开始上传商品。现阶段以完善商品展示和积累实际运营经验为主。

## 相关项目

- RFQ 项目：用于记录、存储和分析 RFQ 报价信息

# Alibaba.com Store

## Project Background

In July 2026, I opened an Alibaba.com store and began using the international B2B platform to present jewelry products and learn about overseas customer demand.

## Project Goals

- Establish an online channel for presenting jewelry products
- Improve product information and the store's basic content
- Receive inquiries and understand overseas market demand
- Accumulate experience for future quotations, customer communication, and product adjustments

## Main Products

The products currently uploaded are mainly jewelry, including:

- Stud earrings
- Necklaces
- Pendants
- Finished jewelry
- Semi-finished jewelry components

## Current Status

The store has been opened and product uploads have begun. At this stage, the focus is on improving the product presentation and accumulating practical operating experience.

## Related Project

- RFQ project: used to record, store, and analyze RFQ quotation information
