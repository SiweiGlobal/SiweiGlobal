# RFQ 项目

## 项目背景

2026 年 7 月，在开通阿里巴巴国际站店铺后创建 RFQ 项目。随着 RFQ 信息逐渐增加，仅依靠临时浏览和手工记忆不利于长期保存、查找和比较，因此需要建立独立的记录系统。

## 项目目标

- 记录 RFQ 的原始信息和报价内容
- 对 RFQ 数据进行长期存储和整理
- 支持历史信息的查询与比较
- 分析客户需求、产品方向和报价情况

## 记录范围

项目主要保存与 RFQ 报价有关的信息及必要的原始记录。具体客户资料和业务数据保存在项目自身的受控系统中，不存放在本目录。

## 当前状态

项目已经创建，当前处于持续记录和完善阶段。后续将根据实际使用情况逐步调整信息结构、存储方式和分析方法。

# RFQ Project

## Project Background

The RFQ project was created in July 2026 after the Alibaba.com store was opened. As RFQ information accumulates, temporary browsing and memory alone are not suitable for long-term preservation, retrieval, or comparison. A dedicated recording system is therefore needed.

## Project Goals

- Record original RFQ information and quotation details
- Store and organize RFQ data over the long term
- Support searches and comparisons of historical information
- Analyze customer demand, product directions, and quotation activity

## Record Scope

The project mainly preserves information related to RFQ quotations and the necessary original records. Specific customer information and business data are kept in the project's own controlled system and are not stored in this directory.

## Current Status

The project has been created and is currently in an ongoing recording and improvement stage. Its information structure, storage method, and analysis approach will be adjusted gradually based on actual use.
